## Organize Your Life in a Flow

To-Do Blocks allows you to organize your daily activities into a sequential flow. Forget about strict deadlines and overwhelming schedules—focus on one task at a time.

The top block is your immediate priority. Complete it to move it to the bottom and reveal your next task. Work at your own pace; if you can't finish a block, postpone it or skip to another. There are no rules, only your peace of mind.

### Features
- **Drag and Drop**: seamlessly reorder your task blocks.
- **Customization**: personalize blocks with names, colors, icons, and notes.
- **Archive & Restore**: keep your workspace tidy without losing history.
- **Theming**: full support for both Light and Dark modes.

**Privacy-focused:** no account is required. All data is stored locally on the device.
