## What's new in the new version and its release date

We're almost there!

_Our Hero! First_ has been thoroughly tested by our beta testers and, after some final polishing, it's ready to launch on Steam, itch.io, and GameJolt!

A huge thank-you goes out to:

- [D4rw1ll](https://www.twitch.tv/d4rv1ll)
- Soraku
- Simone Trapassi
- Zoro the Gallade
- Carlo Mercadante

for playing through every corner of this new version and reporting every little detail. Your help was invaluable.

The release is set for

**May 31, 2026**

There's just one thing left to do: the new game trailer. I've been working on it carefully over the past few days alongside Martin, because I want it to live up to everything else.

### Steam Achievements

The Steam version of the game will support Steam Achievements, **25 in total**, all waiting to be unlocked. Whether you're a completionist or a casual player, there's something here for you!

### Native Builds & Steam Deck Support

Windows, Linux, or Steam Deck? Doesn't matter. _Our Hero! First_ will run natively on all three platforms, with full Steam Deck support, ready to earn the official Verified badge. PC controllers are also recognized automatically, no extra configuration needed.

### High Resolution

The game will run in high resolution, fullscreen, making use of every pixel on your display. Not a given for a title built with RPG Maker VX, but we made it happen (thanks to mkxp-z!)

### Visual Improvements

As detailed in the [previous devlog](/devlog/ourhero-hd-graphics-visual-overhaul), the game received a comprehensive visual overhaul: new character sprites, new animations, lighting, shadows, redesigned HUDs, more readable message fonts, a new logo, and new illustrations. It will feel like an almost entirely different game, and that's not even everything!

### New Mechanics & Improved Gameplay

A number of small changes that make the experience feel smoother and more satisfying:

- you can now dash at any time simply by running (previously only possible during battles, by pressing a specific button)
- added a quick-swap shortcut to toggle between the current weapon and the previously equipped one
- special sword abilities are now assigned automatically (previously required manual selection from the menu)
- increased player movement speed
- rebalanced stats for swords, abilities, enemies, and bosses
- improved game menu: it now displays the current map name and lets you visit the Trophy Hall directly
- and much more****!****

### Drastically Improved Performance

This might be the most impressive change of all. Maps that previously ran at 5-6 FPS now run at a locked **60 FPS**, with no drops whatsoever. See the before-and-after below, it speaks for itself:

### German Localization

German is now officially supported! _Our Hero! First_ can be played in five languages: **Italian, English, Spanish, French, and German**. A special thank-you to D4rw1ll, who proofread the German localization and streamed the entire game during beta testing, you can find the VOD on his YouTube channel: [Part 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bmIceWabUrI) · [Part 2](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=UHVzPzucsEE)
