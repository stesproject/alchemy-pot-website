## Where to get it

You can find the game on **Steam**, **itch.io**, and **GameJolt**.

The **Steam** version includes two exclusive extras:
- 🏆 **25 Steam Achievements** waiting to be unlocked
- ☁️ **Steam Cloud** to automatically sync your saves across devices

Don't have Steam? No problem! The game is available on **itch.io** and **GameJolt** for both **Windows** and **Linux**, with the exact same features, the same quality, and the same price: **free**.

## Is it worth playing (again)?

Yes, and I'm not just saying that because I made it 😄

This isn't a simple patch or a cosmetic update: it's a complete overhaul of the original game. New sprites, new animations, dramatically improved performance, reworked mechanics.
If you've already played it, it'll feel like a brand new title. If you've never tried it, now is the perfect time to do it!

Moreover, **it runs about 3 hours**: just the right length for a weekend session!

And if you play it and enjoy it, I have one small ask: **leave a review**, or update the one you wrote in the past.

For an indie developer, every single review makes a huge difference, not just for visibility, but for the motivation to keep going. And there's still plenty of work ahead, because...


## What's next?

### Next goal: Steam Deck Verified

Steam Deck support is already in place, but I want to earn the official **"Verified"** badge for Our Hero! First. I'll be working on that over the next few days.

### Our Hero! Two: already in the works

I'll now shift focus to the next chapter of the trilogy. This time the approach will be gradual. No waiting months for a single monolithic release:

1. **First release:** mkxp-z integration → HD support, Windows, Linux, and Steam Deck available right away
2. **Follow-up updates:** reworked mechanics, new character art, Steam Achievements

The good news is that most of the work done for *Our Hero! First* is already reusable and can be adapted for the next two games in much less time.

Without making promises I can't keep (but with reasonable optimism) **I'm aiming to complete the Definitive Edition of Our Hero! Two and Our Hero! Omega in about one month per game**. Two months, and the trilogy will be complete. 👀

We'll see how it goes. I'll give it everything I've got!
