## A hero by chance

Ourhero is just a regular guy. Not a hero chosen by fate, not the subject of an ancient prophecy, just someone who was in the wrong place at the right time, and ended up with a noble title and a sword in his hand. But the Teorhemas Crystal has been stolen, the villainous Finalboss has dark plans to conquer the Kingdom, and someone has to stop him. That someone is you!

<Video src={oh1a} class="my-0"/>

## A world to explore

Forests, valleys, water cities, scorching deserts, volcanoes and glaciers: every level is a world of its own, packed with enemies to fight, puzzles to solve and secrets hiding around every corner. Along the way you'll meet a cast of quirky, loveable characters, each with something to say... or something to ask of you!

<Video src={oh1b} class="my-0"/>

## Fight, collect, upgrade

Combat is real-time and the pace is relentless. Your weapon of choice? Swords! And there are plenty to find. Collect them, upgrade them and unlock their special powers, including ranged attacks that will completely change your combat style!

<Video src={oh1c} class="my-0"/>

## There's always more to find

Side quests, hidden collectibles, secret areas waiting to be discovered. Our Hero! First rewards every curious player who dares to look around. For those who want to explore every corner, there's plenty to keep you busy!

<Video src={oh1d} class="my-0"/>

## A handcrafted world

Every character was conceived and hand-drawn by the developer. A world of bipedal cats with a lighthearted, tongue-in-cheek spirit that never takes itself too seriously... and is proud of it!

<Video src={oh1e} class="my-0"/>

## The beginning of something bigger

Our Hero! First is the first chapter of a trilogy. Ourhero doesn't know it yet, but his story has only just begun, and it's going to be far more absurd than he ever expected!
